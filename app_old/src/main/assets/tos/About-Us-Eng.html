About Us

i-women – inspiring women is a free mobile application that delivers a dynamic, interactive stream of real life stories of inspiration written by women from Myanmar and from across the world to women and girls living in Myanmar’s rural areas. i-women app seeks to inspire, foster self-belief in, and channel peer support to rural women to become leaders in their communities.

UNDP Myanmar has been working to support women’s empowerment at local level through federations of Self-Reliant Groups (SRGs) which are expanding the role of women and girls in community development and local governance. The federation spans 2000 village women’s groups in 8 states and regions and connects over 22,500 women.

i-women – inspiring women believes that inspiration is an essential component for the self-development of rural women – “if she can do it, I can do it’’. At the end of each inspirational story shared, there will be a couple of open-ended values based questions, which will spark an on-line and off-line debate on how to take actions based on the inspiration received.

i-women – inspiring women

believes in the sequence that inspiration leads to thought and discussion, which then formulates into plans and actions. The

i-women app

will have several additional resources to support rural women to take their thoughts and discussion from plan to action.
